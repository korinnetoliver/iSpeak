# User Research Observations

## Observation 1

- **Observation Statement:**  
The majority of users said they want athlete voices for competitive motivation.

- **Supporting Data:**  
In the focus group survey, 38% of respondents selected the option indicating they preferred motivational messages from professional athletes. The next closest was 27% for calm mentors.

- **Team Action (if any):**  
Ensure professional athlete's voices are included in our available list.

## Observation 2

- **Observation Statement:** Users are split on whether hearing motivation from someone they admire would help them finish workouts, with “maybe” and “not sure” being the most common responses.   
 

- **Supporting Data:**  When asked "would hearing motivation in the voice/style of someone you admire increase your likelihood of finishing a workout” responses varied. Yes significantly16% maybe 22% not sure 19% “probably not 27% and definitely not 16%


- **Team Action (if any):**  We could offer free trail or demo of the voice feature so skeptical users can experience it before commenting

## Observation 3

- **Observation Statement:**  A majority of users own a fitness tracker and use it at least once a week. 


- **Supporting Data:** Within the focus group survey, 58.3% of users are found to have a fitness tracker app, and  52.8% of users use that fitness tracker app at least once a week. 


-  **Team Action (if any):**  Have a streak feature that notifies users when they add onto it every day or reach a certain amount, and remind them when they are about to lose their streak. 

## Observation 4

- **Observation Statement:** The majority of our user base is time-limited

- **Supporting Data:** The survey results reported that 83.3% of respondents were time-limited college students.


-  **Team Action (if any):** We should put some of our focus on features that help the user manage their time, such as schedule tracking and workout reminders.
